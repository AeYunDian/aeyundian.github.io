# Privacy Policy
## 1. Introduction
Welcome to AeYunDian's undz (website: undz.cn). We take the privacy of our visitors very seriously. This Privacy Policy document aims to explain the types of information we collect and record and how we use it.

## 2. Log Files
Like many other websites, this blog follows the standard procedure of using log files. These files record information when visitors access the website. The information collected includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

## 3. Cookies and Web Beacons
We use "cookies" to store visitors' preference information and the pages they visited on the website. This information is used to optimize users' experience by customizing webpage content based on visitors' browser type or other information.

## 4. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google is one of the third-party vendors on our website. It also uses a technology called the DART cookie to serve ads to our visitors based on their visits to our website and other sites on the internet. Visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com
