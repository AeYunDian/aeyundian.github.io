# Cookie Policy

## Introduction

This document is intended to inform users about the technologies used by this Website, which help to achieve the purposes described below. These technologies allow the Owner to access and store information on the user's device (for example, by using Cookies) or to use resources on the user's device (for example, by running scripts) when the user interacts with this Website.

For simplicity, all such technologies are defined as "Trackers" within this document, unless otherwise noted.
For example, although Cookies can be used in web and mobile browsers, it is not accurate to discuss Cookies in the context of mobile applications, as they are browser-based trackers. Therefore, the term "Cookie" is used in this document only when specifically referring to that type of tracker.

Some uses of Trackers may require the user's consent. Once given, consent can be freely withdrawn at any time in accordance with the instructions provided in this document.

This Website uses first-party Trackers, as well as third-party advertising and analytics services (such as Google AdSense and Baidu Statistics).
The validity and expiration periods of first-party Cookies and other similar Trackers may vary depending on the lifetime set by the Owner. Some of them expire at the end of the user's browsing session.

## How to Manage Preferences and Provide or Withdraw Consent on This Website

When the use of Trackers requires user consent, users can provide or withdraw such consent by setting or updating their preferences via the relevant privacy choice panel provided on this Website.

### How to Control or Delete Cookies and Similar Technologies via Device Settings

Users can use their browser settings to:

- View which Cookies or other similar technologies have been set on their device;
- Block Cookies or similar technologies;
- Clear Cookies or similar technologies from their browser.

However, browser settings do not allow for granular control of consent by category.

For example, users can find information on how to manage Cookies in the most commonly used browsers at the following addresses:

[Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en&p=cpn_cookies)
[Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
[Apple Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/)
[Microsoft Internet Explorer](https://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-vista/block-or-allow-cookies)
[Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4027947)
[Brave](https://support.brave.com/hc/en-us/articles/360022806212-How-do-I-use-Shields-while-browsing)
[Opera](https://help.opera.com/en/latest/web-preferences/#cookies)

Users may also manage certain categories of Trackers used on mobile websites by opting out through relevant device settings (e.g., advertising settings or general tracking settings on mobile devices – users can open their device settings and look for the relevant option).

### Consequences of Blocking Trackers

However, please note that Trackers help this Website provide users with a better experience and advanced features (consistent with the purposes outlined in this document). Therefore, if users choose to block the use of Trackers, the Owner may be unable to provide the related features.
