# About

## Site Introduction
- **Site Name**: undz
- **Launch Date**: 2025-02-22

## Technical Implementation
- **Domain Names**: This site uses two domain names, `undz.cn` and `io.hb.cn`. Both domains are registered through Alibaba Cloud Intelligence Technology (Hangzhou) Co., Ltd., with DNS resolution and SSL certificates provided by Cloudflare.
- **Certificates**: Both domains hold legitimate domain certificates issued by the China Internet Network Information Center (CNNIC). Registrant information has been verified through real-name authentication.
- **Security & Encryption**:
  - The entire site uses HTTPS encrypted access.
  - SSL certificates are provided by [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/).
- Powered by [VuePress](https://vuepress.vuejs.org/)
- **Icons**: The site icon was designed by myself. All other vector icons use Font Awesome 6 Solid from Iconify, authored by Dave Gandy.
- **Theme**: [vuepress-theme-hope](https://theme-hope.vuejs.press/)
