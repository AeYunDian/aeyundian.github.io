# About

## Site Info

## Technical Implementation

- **Domain**: This site uses two domains, `undz.cn` and `io.hb.cn`. Both domains are registered through Alibaba Cloud Intelligence Technology (Hangzhou) Co., Ltd., and DNS resolution and SSL certificates are provided by Cloudflare.
- **Certificate**: Both domains hold legitimate domain certificates issued by the China Internet Network Information Center (CNIC). Registrant information has been verified through real-name authentication.
- **Security & Encryption**:
  - The entire site uses HTTPS encrypted access.
  - SSL certificates are provided by [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/).
- Powered by [VuePress](https://vuepress.vuejs.org/)
- Icons: The site icon is designed by myself. All other vector icons use Iconify's Font Awesome 6 Solid, authored by Dave Gandy.
- Theme: [vuepress-theme-hope](https://theme-hope.vuejs.press/)
